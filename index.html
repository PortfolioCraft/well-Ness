About Me

Hi there! I am a Doctor of Nursing Practice and dually Board-Certified Psychiatric and Mental Health Nurse Practitioner and Family Nurse Practitioner. I believe in the balance of spiritual, mental, social, physical, and emotional well-being and I go above and beyond by making sure they all align in my treatment approach.

I am a motivator, educator, and advocate for my patients and take much pride in doing such roles. Health is wealth and it all starts with a healthy mental state. My motto is “Be kind to your mind.”

My Story

I started my journey as a Registered Nurse earning my associate's degree in 2006. I continued to advance my education by pursuing my Bachelor’s of Science degree in nursing in 2009 from Alcorn State University located in Natchez Mississippi. I went back to Alcorn State University in 2014 to earn my Master of Science in Nursing as a Family Nursing Practitioner.


I decided to continue to further my education and received a post-masters in Psychiatric Mental Health Nurse Practitioner in 2018 from Midwestern State University located in Wichita Falls Texas. I completed my Doctor of Nursing Practice (DNP) at Wilmington University in New Castle, Delaware in July 2023.

Holistic wellness recognizes that mental health is interconnected with various aspects of life. I believe that achieving a balance between spiritual, mental, social, physical, and emotional well-being is crucial for mental health. By addressing these aspects together, we can promote lasting positive changes and a better quality of life.
+
For clients dealing with anxiety or depression, I take a personalized approach. Therapy sessions may involve cognitive-behavioral techniques, mindfulness practices, and other evidence-based therapies. Additionally, I may prescribe medications if necessary, always keeping in mind the holistic well-being of the individual.
